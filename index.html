Who's Ye?

So, technically, Mr. West sometimes refers to himself a "Ye," but he has yet to copyright the term and I've since decided to embody it.

My name is Erick Manrique, I am a student at Thinkful's Software Engineer Immersion Program.

Originally born in Argentina, I was raised on both sides of the Hudson River between NYC and New Jersey. Currently based in South Florida, I am never to far from the beach, the city, or the wildlife (Florida Man included).

What Ye Likes

After researching their claims on my own, I found they weren't kidding. Software development will play an foundational role in a society that's rapidly diving into technology and the future.

I had to see if I had what it takes, so I started dabbling on freeCodeCamp and found myself pretty fascinated. I had to dive deeper, so deep, I ended up here, building my very first webpage!
